About

The Noah3D dataset is a large-scale autonomous driving dataset with 2D&3D object annotations.

• 1 Million LiDAR frames, 4 Million camera images

• 200 km^2 driving regions, 144 driving hours

• 15k fully annotated scenes with 5 classes (Car, Bus, Truck, Pedestrian, Cyclist)

• Diverse environments (day/night, sunny/rainy, urban/suburban areas)

Sensors

The data acquisition vehicle is equipped with 1 64-beam LiDAR on the top and 7 cameras.
